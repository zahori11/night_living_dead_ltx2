# 🖼️ Visual Resources

Welcome to the Art and Postproduction Department! 🎨

This folder acts as our media archive (Media Pool). All graphic materials, keyframes, and generated image sequences that make up the film are stored here before final editing.

## 📁 Directory Contents

* **`/frames`**: Stylized keyframes used across the **22 scenes (2s each)**. The first, middle and last frames of every scene are taken from the **original footage and then stylized in 1-bit pixel art (Seedream / Freepik)**. The last frame of each scene is reused as the starting keyframe of the following scene to maintain visual continuity. These frames act as anchors for the AI animation.
  
* **`/assets`** (e.g., CRT / Arcade Frames): Static set-design elements generated by AI (such as retro televisions or arcade machines) used as masks or overlays in the editing suite to frame the final footage.

## ⚙️ Postproduction Notes

To maintain the retro **"Game Boy Noir"** aesthetic, remember that any scaling applied to these images in your editing software must use the **"Nearest Neighbor"** (pixel replication) interpolation method or **"Hard Edges"**. Never use smoothing (bicubic interpolation), or you will destroy the perfectly crisp pixels!
